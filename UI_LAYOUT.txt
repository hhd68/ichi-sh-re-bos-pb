║                   CONDITION DETAILS                           ║
╚═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╝

1. BIAS (HTF):
   BUY when ALL true:
   • Close > max(SSA, SSB)
   • Tenkan > Kijun
   • SSA > SSB
   • Chikou > max(SSA[26], SSB[26])
   • Chikou > Close[26]
   
   SELL: Mirror conditions (all <)

2. SH (MTF):
   BUY: Swing high fake break + return
   • Find swing high (fractal)
   • Break above (≥1 close above)
   • Return below swing high
   
   SELL: Swing low fake break + return

3. RE (MTF):
   Within 5 candles after SH:
   BUY: No close < SH zone low
   SELL: No close > SH zone high

4. BOS (LTF):
   Lookback 5 bars (bars 2-6):
   BUY: Close[1] > HH
   SELL: Close[1] < LL

5. PB (LTF):
   Within 5 candles:
   Price touches/enters BOS zone
